Our Mission

Empowering young girls to embrace their uniqueness, build confidence, and practice self-care through personalized beauty and wellness kits.

Why We Started Swervy Cares

In a world where young girls are constantly bombarded with unrealistic beauty standards and social pressures, we saw a need for a different approach. Swervy Cares was born from the belief that every girl deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and valued for who she truly is.

Our founder recognized that self-care isn't just about looking good‚Äîit's about feeling good from the inside out. It's about taking time for yourself, celebrating your individuality, and building the confidence to face the world with your head held high.

Our Founder's Story

"Growing up, I struggled with self-confidence like many young girls do. I wished I had someone to tell me that I was beautiful just the way I was, and that taking care of myself was an act of self-love, not vanity."

"When I became a mother, I realized I wanted to create something that would help other girls feel the confidence I wished I had. Swervy Cares is my way of reaching out to every girl who needs a reminder that she is worthy of love and care‚Äîespecially her own."

- Sarah Johnson, Founder

Our Impact Goals

We're committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of young girls. Here's what we're working toward:

  • 500 Kits by 2025: Deliver personalized self-care kits to girls who need them most
  • Community Partnerships: Work with schools, community centers, and youth organizations
  • Educational Programs: Develop workshops on self-care, confidence, and body positivity
  • Mentorship Network: Connect young girls with positive role models and mentors
  • Sustainable Growth: Build a self-sustaining organization that can continue this mission for years to come
